## Overview

Compare current behavior to historical data (week over week, month over month). Answer questions like "how is this week compared to last week?"

## Time Windows

- Day vs. Day
- Week vs. Week
- Month vs. Month
- Age-based comparisons

## Metrics Tracked

**Sleep**: Total per day, average nap duration, longest session, night wakings
**Feeding**: Feeds per day, interval between feeds, volume
**Diapers**: Total changes, wet vs. dirty ratio

## Success Metrics

- Trends are easy to understand
- Questions about "how are we doing" are answered well
- Data helps inform decisions
